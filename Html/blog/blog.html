ARTICLE 1: WHAT ARE AAGAS?

Aagas mean green warriors meaning protectors of the nature. Aagaas are aerobic hot pile decentralised bulk waste composter that can carry a load of around 20KG of biodegradable waste each. The main advantge of this method of disposal is that the contents do not need to be mixed, stirred or shifted. It can be installed at terraces, gardens, or other open spaces.

DIRECTIONS FOR USE:

  • 1) ORGANIC/BIODEGRADABLE WASTE IS TO BE ADDED INTO THE COMPOSTER TILL ITS NECK (AROUND 18-20 KGS OF WASTE).
  • 2) LAYER WITH ADEQUATE REMIX POWDER (SPECIALISED MICRO-ORGANISMS CONTAINING POWDER THAT HAS HIGH EFFICIENCY.
  • 3) THIS POWDER ALSO HELPS IN SMELL FREE COMPOSTING.
  • 4) LEAVE THE AAGA DISTURBED FOR THE FIRST 4 WEEKS.
  • 5) HARVEST COMPOST AFTER 4 WEEKS FOR THE FIRST CYCLE AND THEN EVERY 2 WEEKS.
